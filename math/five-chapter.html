Q.1 একটি সমান্তর প্রগতির \(n^{th}\) তম পদ \(a_n= 3 + 4n\) হইলে, সাধারণ অন্তর(d) = ?

(A) \( 3\)
(B) \( 4 \)
(C) \( 0 \)
(D) \( -2 \)
Explanation
Correct Ans:(b)
$$ \def\bijusH#1{\require{cancel}{\cancel{#1}}} \require{enclose} \begin{aligned} a_{n+1}&=3+4(n+1)\\ &=7 + 4n \\ a_n &=3 + 4n(given)\\ d &= a_{n+1} - a_n \\ &= 7 +\bijusH{ 4n} - 3 + \bijusH{4n}\\ &= 4 \end{aligned} $$

(B) \(\sqrt {346}\)
(C)\(\sqrt {500}\)
(D) \(\sqrt {200}\)
Explanation
Ans:(d):
$$ \def\bijusH#1{\require{cancel}{\cancel{#1}}} \require{enclose} \begin{aligned} n &= 10 \\ a_1 &= \sqrt 2 \\ a_2 &= \sqrt 8\\ d &=a_2 - a_1\\ &= \sqrt{4×2} - \sqrt 2\\ &= 2\sqrt{2} - \sqrt 2\\ &= \sqrt 2 \\ a_n&=a +(n-1)d \\ a_{10}&= \sqrt 2 +(10-1)\sqrt 2 \\ &=\sqrt 2+9\sqrt 2\\ &=10\sqrt 2\\ & \therefore a_{10} = \sqrt{200}\\ \end{aligned} $$

(13) 24, 21, 18, ...... এই সমাস্তর প্রগতিটির কতটি পদ নিলে তাদের যোগফল 78 হবে ?
H.S.L.C 2023
(a) 4 এবং 16
(b) 6 এবং 11
(c) 5 এবং 12
(d) 4 এবং 13
Explanation
Ans: (d) :
$$ \def\bijusH#1{\require{cancel}{\cancel{#1}}} \require{enclose} \begin{aligned} s_n &= 78 \\ a_1 &= 24 \\ a_2 &= 21\\ d &=a_2 - a_1\\ &= 21 - 24 \\ &= -3 \\ s_n &= \frac{n}{2}[2a + (n -1)d]\\ 78 &= \frac{n}{2}[2× 24 + (n -1)(-3)]\\ 156 &= n[48 - 3n + 3]\\ &\Rightarrow 3n^2 -51n +156 = 0 \\ &\Rightarrow 3(n^2 -17n +52) = 0\\ &\Rightarrow n^2 -17n +52 = 0 \\ &\Rightarrow (n -13)(n - 4) = 0 \\ & \therefore n = 13,4 \end{aligned} $$
(14) \(-6, -\frac{11}{2},-5,...\)এই সমাস্তর প্রগতিটির কতটি পদ নিলে তাদের যোগফল -25 হবে ?

(a) \(4,6 \)
(b) \(20 \)
(c) \(5, 20 \)
(d) \(7,8 \)
Explanation
Ans: (c):
$$ \def\bijusH#1{\require{cancel}{\cancel{#1}}} \require{enclose} \begin{aligned} s_n &= -25 \\ a_1 &= -6 \\ a_2 &= -\frac{11}{2}\\ d &=a_2 - a_1\\ &= -6 - (-\frac{11}{2}) \\ &= \frac{1}{2} \\ s_n &= \frac{n}{2}[2a + (n -1)d]\\ -25 &= \frac{n}{2}[-12+ (n -1)(\frac{1}{2})]\\ -50 &= \frac{-24n +n^2 -n}{2}\\ &\Rightarrow n^2 -25n +100 = 0\\ &\Rightarrow (n -20)(n - 5) = 0 \\ & \therefore n = 20,5 \end{aligned} $$
(15) সমষ্টি নির্ণয় করো
\( 999\frac{1}{7}\)+\(999\frac{2}{7}\)+\(999\frac{3}{7}\)+\(999\frac{4}{7}\)+\(999\frac{5}{7}\)+\(999\frac{6}{7} \) = ?

(A) \( 3997 \)
(B) \( 2995 \)
(C) \( 2992 \)
(D) \( 2997 \)
Explanation
Ans: (d):
$$ \def\bijusH#1{\require{cancel}{\cancel{#1}}} \require{enclose} \begin{aligned} s_n &= \frac{n}{2}(a + l)\\ \therefore s_n &= \ ? \\ a &= 999\frac{1}{7} \\ l &= 999\frac{6}{7} \\ n &= 6 \\ s_6 &={ \frac{\bijusH{6}}{\bijusH{2}}}^3(999\frac{1}{7} + 999\frac{6}{7})\\ &=3 × 999(\frac{1}{7} + \frac{6}{7})\\ &=3 × 999({\frac{\bijusH{7}}{\bijusH{7}}}^1)\\ &=3 × 999 \\ &= 2997\\ &\therefore Ans \ = 2997 \end{aligned} $$
(16) \( 2,4,6,8....\) প্রগতিটির কত সংখ্যক পদে 50 থাকবে ?
HSLC 2023

(a) \(26\)
(b) \(28\)
(c) \(29\)
(d) \(25\)
Explanation
Ans: (d):
$$ \def\bijusH#1{\require{cancel}{\cancel{#1}}} \require{enclose} \begin{aligned} a_n &=50 \\ a_1&= 2\\ a_2&= 4\\ d &=a_2 - a_1\\ d &= 4-2\\ d &= 2\\ \therefore a_n &= a +(n-1)d\\ 50 &= 2 + (n -1)2\\ 50 &= \bijusH{2} + 2n \bijusH{-2}\\ &\Rightarrow \bijusH{2}n =\bijusH{50}^{25}\\ n&=25\\ &\therefore Ans \ =25 \end{aligned} $$
