About Bijush Academy

Bijush Academy is an educational platform designed especially for SEBA (Board of Secondary Education, Assam) students in classes 9 and 10. We aim to provide structured, interactive, and easy-to-understand learning resources across subjects like Mathematics, Science, English, and more.

Our platform stands out for its simple UI, gamified learning experience, and regularly updated resources such as NCERT Solutions, previous year papers, and general knowledge sections. Everything is crafted with students in mind to boost their academic performance and confidence.

Founded by a passionate educator, Bijush Academy believes that every student deserves access to high-quality education, regardless of location or background.
